Criminology



The Bachelor of Science in Criminology(BS Crim) is a four-year degree program in CCDC that is mainly concerened with the nature of many crimes and criminals. Taking this course will help you understand what is crime, how do crimes occur, and what makes people more likely to commit crime or be a victim of it. It will also teach you how the justice system works, how alleged and convicted criminals should be handled, and the methods that can be used to reform them.


Vision

The College of Criminal Justice Education envisions to produce graduates who are morally, mentally, physically, and spiritually, upright towards their career in the field of Criminal Justice.


Mission

The College of Criminal Justice Education shall produce graduates who are geared towards Holistic Development, dynamic and competent leaders in the field of Law Enforcement Administration, Criminal Sociology, Crime Detection and Investigation, Forensic Science and Correctional Administration.


Strategies: This course is designed to:

1. foster the faith of the student to the Almighty God, a higher concept of leadership integrity, and profound understanding of one’s moral and legal responsibilities as a citizen;


2. Embibe the criminology ethics and motivate the students to live with it


3. equip the students with proficiency and competence in the field of public safety, law enforcement, correctional administration, and scientific crime detection essential to the machinery of Criminal Justice System based on the principles of respect to human rights and due process of law.


Our objectives is to developed higher concepts of citizenry, accountability, and Christian Leadership, and understanding of one’s moral responsibility to his fellowmen, his community, and his country
